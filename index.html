About us

Why CookingwithChibbs? 🤷

  • Fun, exciting, and entertaining
  • Opportunity for placements
  • Flexible timetable

Cooking with Chibbs is your one stop site for learning how to prepare exquisite continental and local Nigerian dishes.

We also cater for your events such as weddings, birthday parties, dinner, lunch and buffets.

Our ingredients are sourced with you in our minds from the most trusted suppliers in terms of quality. We make bold to say we know our onions when it comes to what we do 😉.
